NIJISANJI Postpones Mario Kart 8 Event

11 Feburary 2024 | by Knoife

NIJISANJI postpones their EN Mario Kart Special Battle on Sunday, 11 Feburary. The company announced the postponement of their tournament citing "To dedicate their efforts to ensure the livers' well-being." The event was scheduled on this date initially, however they cited with the "current situation", in which the whole community took it as a reference with the Selen Tatsuki termination fiasco five days ago. The agency did not go into specifics when the reschedule date was going to take place.
